Project Proposal

Problem Description

  1. Provide an overview of the general topic or question of interest.

    Our project will be developing a study aid web application to help students prepare for exams and quizzes. We hope to create an interactive tool where students can view and create flashcards, as well as take quizzes based on their created materials. As current students, we understand the importance of study tools and are excited to explore this topic.

  2. Identify an information technology problem in that space.

    In todays digital age, students often struggle to find effective (and free!) study tools. Most tools currently out there use paywalls (such as Brainscape) or features that are distracting to work with. Although some offer free versions, they are often limited in functionality. We hope to make an easily accessible, non-distracting, and functional application to address this issue

  3. Identify at least 1 previous or existing attempt to solve this problem

    One existing attempt to address this problem is Quizlet, a popular study platform that allows students to create flashcards and practice quizzes. While Quizlet is widely used, many of its most helpful features—such as advanced study modes and offline access— are locked behind a paywall. Additionally, ads and extra features can make the interface distracting. Our project aims to provide a simpler, free, and more focused alternative for students who want effective study tools without unnecessary barriers.

Proposed Application

  1. Who are the users of the application? That is, who will be accessing the website? What impacts might this app have on minority groups?

    The primary users of this application are students, including high school and college students who are preparing for exams, quizzes, and other assessments. The app is designed for anyone who benefits from structured studying, regardless of academic background or subject area.

    By offering a free and easy-to-use study tool, this app may especially benefit students from lower-income or underrepresented backgrounds who may not have access to paid study platforms. Additionally, allowing users to create their own study materials supports different learning styles, which can help students with diverse educational needs.

  2. What will the users do with the web app? What kind of interactive features are you expecting to provide?

    Users will be able to create, view and organize flashcards based on their study topics. Once flashcards are created, users can navigate through them and will be able to flip each card to reveal the answer. Additionally, users can also take quizzes based off their creating flashcards and receive feedback on their performance.

    The main interactive features will include:

    • The ability to create, edit and organize flashcards that users can cycle through to study. Will also have a feature where users can time themselves going through the flashcard sets and then get stats based on their performance during that time period.

    • Users will be able to play a quiz game based off a selected flashcard set (or multiple depending on user preference) where they will be presented with questions that they will need to answer. As the user gets questions correct, their character will progress toward an objective (there will be two checkpoints on the way to this objective). Once the user has reached the objective, they will receive a reward screen with some sort of prize for completing the quiz, a congratulatory message, and stats on their quiz performance.

    • Users can organize their flashcards by course and have individual flashcard sets for specific topics within each course. They will also be able to select multiple flashcard sets to study or use all sets within a given course.

  3. What kind of information will the users be viewing?

    Users will view study materials that they create themselves, including flashcards organized by course and topic. Each flashcard will contain a question or concept and its respective answer hidden. Users can then navigate through their flashcards, flipping them to reveal answers.

    Our app will also include quiz features using information the user provides. During quizzes, users will see their selected answers, correct answers, and receive a score on how well they perform.

    All information shown in the app will be user-generated and stored using cache or in a database. No API service is needed for our features.

  4. How will using this app help to solve your chosen problem?

    By providing a free, user-friendly website for students to create and use study materials, we can address the need for effective study tools that aren't behind paywalls. Our site will allow students of any income level to tailor their studying needs to their own preferences while still allowing them to successfully learn and prepare for exams. This ensures that no student is left behind or disadvantaged in their education simply because they cannot afford expensive study platform subscriptions.
